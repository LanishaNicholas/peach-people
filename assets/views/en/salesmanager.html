Join Our Team as a Sales Manager

We are looking for a passionate and talented manager to join our team.

Company : Peach People

Location: Norway, Sweden

Peach People is on the lookout for a seasoned Sales Manager with a proven track record to bolster our dynamic sales team. We need a strategic thinker who can harness their experience to drive sales, mentor our team, and spot opportunities in the market. If you're a proactive leader with a knack for numbers and relationships, Peach People is the place for you.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Oversees the sales department and its daily operations.
  • Develops and implements strategic sales plans tailored to company goals.
  • Utilizes past experience to identify potential sales opportunities and areas of improvement.
  • Sets sales targets and quotas, ensuring they align with business objectives.
  • Coaches, mentors, and trains sales representatives to improve performance.
  • Monitors sales metrics and analyzes data to adjust strategies as needed.
  • Manages and fosters relationships with key clients and business partners.
  • Evaluates market trends and competitor activities to identify threats and opportunities.
  • Collaborates with marketing and other departments to align strategies and campaigns.
  • Uses experience to anticipate challenges and proactively address them.
  • Ensures the sales team adheres to company policies, values, and ethical standards.

Qualifications:

  • Proven experience as a Sales Manager or similar senior sales role.
  • Ability to analyze sales data and derive actionable insights.
  • Exceptional communication and team management skills.
