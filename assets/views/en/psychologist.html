Join Our Team as a Online Behavior Psychologist

We are looking for a passionate and talented Online Behavior Psychologist to join our team.

Company : Peach Group

Location: Norway

Peach Group is at the cutting edge of understanding online behaviors, and we're seeking an Online Behavior Psychologist to deepen our insights. If you're passionate about understanding the nuances of online user behavior, leveraging psychological principles to enhance user experience, and want to be a part of a dynamic team making waves in the digital realm, then Peach Group is your next platform.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Analyzes online user behavior, identifying patterns, trends, and key insights.
  • Collaborates with UX/UI designers to optimize user experience based on behavioral findings.
  • Designs and conducts experiments to test hypotheses related to online behavior.
  • Provides recommendations to marketing and product teams based on psychological insights.
  • Keeps abreast of the latest research and trends in online behavior psychology.
  • Assists in creating content that resonates with target audiences based on psychological principles.
  • Evaluates the effectiveness of interventions and strategies implemented.
  • Offers training and workshops to internal teams on the principles of online behavior.
  • Collaborates with data scientists to interpret and visualize behavioral data.
  • Upholds the ethical standards of psychological research and practice.

Qualifications:

  • Advanced degree in Psychology, Behavioral Science, or a related field.
  • Proven experience in online or digital behavior research.
  • Strong analytical and research skills.
  • Familiarity with online platforms, social media, and digital marketing.
  • Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal.

Join Peach Group and be at the forefront of unlocking the mysteries of online behavior. Apply today!
